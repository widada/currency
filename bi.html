Nisa Counting Machine
The Nisa-branded counting machine was in use at De Javasche Bank to support operating activities. Nisa counting machines were made in Czechoslovakia from iron, copper and ebonite. On the front was a square block holding 100 white and grey keys. On the back was a horizontal opening to place the paper that could slide from left to right as the machine was operated. On the right-hand side were several sliding keys and a power cord.
